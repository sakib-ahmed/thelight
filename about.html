Our Liberty is Bound Together

We can't help everyone but everyone can help someone. So let's make the disadvantaged people become successful by making sure their basic needs are met and securing their lives through providing the opportunity for better education. So we can smile together with the thousands.

To educate the rural people who are helpless - some are in child labour, we will make sure they get their fundamental rights and we will provide their monthly educational expenses, especially to the orphan children. Education is a fundamental right. however there are millions of children who are denied that right.

To eradicate unemployment problem - offering some courses such as computer course, sewing course can reduce poverty and inequality, as it provides people the opportunity to provide for themselves and their children.

To give our society the best service they deserve such as hygienic water, medical aid, disability support etc.

Distribute clothes in remote areas in Bangladesh, particularly winter clothes. In the month of Ramadan, we will endeavour to provide Ramadan food pack and Eid gifts.

To arrange inspirational events for the children here in the UK so they get to know Bangladesh much more and to raise awareness of the social issues that exist

STORY

Our mission is simple, to support underprivileged children around Bangladesh to have a better chance in education and in life. Specially to help prevent child labour and empower young people in developing nations to fulfil their potential through education, vocational training and life skills. So therefore, our fundraising helps give those most in need the very best opportunities to overcome barriers. And we do it with a smile!
